Principal Investigator

Dr. Danielle Law, PhD

Principal Investigator

Associate Professor, Psychology & Youth and Children's Studies

I found my home at Laurier Brantford in 2012 and am very excited to be part of the growth and development of this vibrant, interdisciplinary and collaborative community. I spent most of my childhood and early adulthood in and around Edmonton, Alberta, where I completed my undergraduate education at the University of Alberta. In 2002, I made my way to beautiful Vancouver, British Columbia where I spent 10 years completing my graduate degrees (MA and PhD) and my post-doctoral work.

Research Team

Katherine Reid

PhD Student in Developmental Psychology

Katherine completed her Master's in Developmental Psychology at Wilfrid Laurier University and her Undergraduate Honours in Neuroscience and Mental Health at Carleton University. Her research focuses on parent-child shared book reading and early literacy instruction.

Avnee Sharma

PhD Student in Developmental Psychology

Avnee completed her BA Psychology and MA Developmental Psychology at Wilfrid Laurier University. Her research focuses on understanding the impact of social media influencers on young people's social and emotional development.

Alia Hussain

Master's Student in Developmental Psychology

Alia completed her undergraduate degree in psychology at the University of Western Ontario in 2020 and is currently a second-year Developmental Psychology Master's Student. Her research focuses on youth's social-emotional development, developmental psychopathology, and factors influencing the development of youth with ADHD and autism.
